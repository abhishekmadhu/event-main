About Us

We are a youth run start-up organization in India with student body entities in process of expanding across major cities in India. We have one such established entity in Kolkata, West Bengal which is fully functional now.

The Kolkata entity is also our flagship entity among many more coming up soon! At present we aim to successfully build a strong network of entities across cities to help us expand our impact. With the help of our entities we organize various events like workshops, summits, meetups, etc. Read More

What is Our Goal?

These aim to help us bring out the positivity and change to make lives of people better and happy! Our primary target is the youth of the country – who are the today and tomorrow of the world we live in. They are the ones of whom the modern society will comprise of so it is important to empower them and make them believe in their passion and themselves.

An empowered youth empowers a society and an empowered society empowers a nation – and that is the ultimate picture we want framed!
