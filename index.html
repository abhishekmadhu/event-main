About Us

We are a youth run start-up organization in India with student body entities in process of expanding across major cities in India. We have one such established entity in Kolkata, West Bengal which is fully functional now.

The Kolkata entity is also our flagship entity among many more coming up soon! At present we aim to successfully build a strong network of entities across cities to help us expand our impact. With the help of our entities we organize various events like workshops, summits, meetups, etc.

Our Story

Events and Byond is a thriving entrepreneurial venture dedicated to utilizing the extensive power of the youth to meet the objectives. Our core competency lies in planning and execution mix which includes organizing events, youth connects, conferences, workshops, leadership summits and various youth empowerment programmes and projects,social media and on ground campaigns, videos and content, maintaining relationships online, reaching out to target specific audience, integrating offline goals with online marketing and measuring ROI. Events and Byond prides itself with a small and crisp team of young professionals from diverse backgrounds who can bring fresh ideas and perspective to the table.
